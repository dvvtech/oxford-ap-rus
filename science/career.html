Professional Career CV

At the same time, Prof. Doctor. Yashar Ibadov began his scientific career as a doctor, he was interested in the study of psychology with the goal of serving humanity...

Therefore, he began to conduct in-depth research specifically in the field of clinical psychology. Based on the results of these in-depth studies, he became the author of many methodologies and presented all this information under the title “Alternative Psychology”. A certificate of authorship of this science (No. 6274) was received in Moscow in 2003.

"Alternative Psychology" This is a new area of psychology that can answer questions about solving problems that arise when a person interacts with the categories of time and space. This science studies the transformation of living systems in time and space - from one state to another and studies the style of their movement.

The methods of this science consist of: medical and pedagogical - psychological tests in the form of abstract compositions, psychography, Psychocorrection, Phase portrait - system, etc...

“Alternative Psychology” opens up practically new possibilities for the health of human physical and energetic bodies and promotes scientific research from an alternative point of view, based on the laws of nature, towards the integration of modern sciences.

In 2005, a Eurasian patent (No. 00649) was obtained.
